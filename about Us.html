About Us

Our Story

Virgin Finance was licensed by URSB on 17th October 2024 to offer services like savings and credit. With over 100 clients, it serves many through a one-on-one remote approach.

Its focus is solving financial issues in business and housing using value chain financing, promoting economic growth.
