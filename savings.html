Group Savings Account

Secure your future together with UGAFODE's group savings account tailored for communities.

Product Features

  • Easy group account management
  • Flexible deposit and withdrawal options
  • Secure transactions with full transparency
  • Group members can save together conveniently

Benefits

  • Encourages collective savings culture
  • Improves financial discipline among members
  • Offers access to affordable credit
  • Promotes economic empowerment in communities

Eligibility

  • Groups must have a minimum of 5 members
  • Members must be Ugandan citizens aged 18 and above
  • Groups should have a recognized leadership structure

Requirements

  • Valid identification documents for all members
  • Proof of residence or community affiliation
  • Completed group savings application form
